About Me

Hello, my name is James Triveri.

I’m an Actuarial Consultant at CNA Financial and currently a member of the Reserving Analytics and Insights team. My primary interests are Machine Learning, Scientific Computing and Performance Optimization.

I’m an ardent supporter of Free Software, as well as a long-time user and advocate of the Python Programming Language. I’m an active contributor to Open Source, and maintain a few projects of my own. You can check out my GitHub page here.

I graduated from Augustana College in Rock Island, Illinois where I studied Mathematics and Physics.

My favorite animal is the sloth (the Bradypodidae variety).

My CV can be found here.

Thanks for checking in!
